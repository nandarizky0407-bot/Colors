🗣️ Percakapan Panjang (Review Gabungan)

Alex: "Hello! Good morning. My name is Alex. I am ten years old. What is your name?"
Budi: "Hi Alex! Good morning. My name is Budi. I am nine years old. Nice to meet you."
Alex: "Nice to meet you too, Budi. Wow, look at your bag! It is so cool. It is blue!"
Budi: "Thank you! Yes, my bag is blue. It has three yellow pencils inside."
Alex: "Awesome. I have a red book and two green erasers. Colors are so fun!"
Budi: "You are right. Let's enter the classroom together!"
💡 Misi Siswa: Tutor membaca bagian Alex, siswa membaca bagian Budi. Coba minta siswa menerjemahkan kata yang dicetak tebal spontan!
